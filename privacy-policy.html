Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Asif Digital Studio ("we", "our", or "us") is the parent organization that operates and manages the mobile distribution brand Playful Venture (officially registered under the legal entity Sadia Shahzadi). This universal Privacy Policy directly applies to all users across our full digital application ecosystem, explicitly governing products released under our official storefront identifiers—including Asif Digital Studio, Playful Venture, and Sadia Shahzadi—on the Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

This Policy explicitly details data safety parameters for all our mobile offerings, covering our hyper-casual, horror, driving simulator, and jurassic adventure catalogues, and directly applies to our title Ramadan Adventure: Muslim Life.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you interact with any of our applications and associated services.

Information We Collect

We do not collect highly sensitive personal data (such as your real-world identity, physical address, or direct contact records). To support core gameplay systems, personalize user experiences, and maintain online features, our titles may collect the following information clusters:

  • Player Profiles & Leaderboards: In titles featuring competitive scores, dynamic game progress, or online leaderboards, a unique account identifier or nickname profile is established. Depending on the specific game, this profile may use a randomly generated placeholder configuration or allow for player-customized display names. These identifiers are stored securely via backend cloud systems (such as Firebase) to maintain your online scores, achievements, and player progress.
  • Gameplay Save Data: Progress states, unlocked inventory, high scores, purchased items, or unlocked levels may be saved locally on your device or linked securely to cloud-based server entities.
  • Device type, operating system version, and system hardware specifications.
  • App performance aggregates, gameplay interaction choices, data tracking metrics, and core engagement levels.
  • Crash logs, technical diagnostics, and software troubleshooting variables.
  • Advertising identifiers associated with your system framework (such as IDFA / GAID).
  • Approximate geolocation parameters processed anonymously via IP data routing.

Players visiting from the European Economic Area (EEA), California, or other applicable jurisdictions enjoy clear data privacy choices, including access options, account removal structures, and tracking opt-out channels.

Children’s Privacy

We design our applications to offer safe experiences. We do not knowingly maintain or request personal data strings from children under the age of 13. If any such collection tracks are detected, our operators will execute corrective data purges immediately.

Community Rules & Leaderboard Names

To maintain a safe, respectful, and family-friendly environment for all players, public usernames and display profiles utilized across our multiplayer components or competitive leaderboard lists must adhere to the following community guidelines:

  • No Offensive Content: Profile configurations must not include profanity, vulgarity, explicit themes, hate speech, aggressive statements, or discriminatory phrasing targeting individuals or groups.
  • No Personal Information: For your own tracking safety, do not include real-world personal identification records (such as phone details, real-world full names, email logs, or location tags) in public leaderboards.
  • Enforcement & Reporting: In games featuring competitive scores, players have access to an intuitive in-game reporting mechanism to instantly flag and block entries that violate these rules. We reserve the right to review, restrict, or remove public names and scoring metrics that disrupt our community code of conduct.

Account & Data Deletion Policy

We respect your privacy rights and give you full control over the information we maintain. In accordance with modern mobile store operator compliance requirements, you have the right to demand the absolute, permanent deletion of your profile history, scoring markers, and associated game records at any time.

How to Request Data Deletion:

Because our systems optimize data architecture without linking identifying user accounts, profile clear operations are handled manually by our service team. Please submit an application request to:

Email: playfulventuregames@gmail.com

Subject Line: Data Deletion Request

Required Details: Please state the specific game title, along with your custom player name or internal unique User/Player ID, so we can confidently locate and clear your corresponding storage records from our database platforms.

Processing Timeline: Once a validated operational request arrives via email, our team will manually clear your document files, scoring metrics, cloud progress entries, and unique hardware correlation IDs across our database platforms within 7 business days. This structural removal process is absolute and cannot be undone.
