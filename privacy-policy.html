Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Asif Digital Studio ("we", "our", or "us") develops and publishes games and digital applications for platforms including Apple App Store, Google Play Store, and Steam.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use our applications and services.

Information We Collect

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information such as names or addresses. Our applications may automatically collect non-personal information including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA / GAID)
  • Approximate location (IP-based)

Advertising Partners

Our apps display ads provided by third-party ad networks. These partners may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — Privacy Policy
  • Unity Ads — Privacy Policy
  • AppLovin — Privacy Policy
  • Meta (Facebook) Audience Network — Privacy Policy

Analytics & Usage Data

We may use analytics tools to understand how users interact with our applications. This data is collected anonymously and used to improve performance and stability.

GDPR & CCPA Rights

Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) or California have rights regarding their data, including access, deletion, and opting out of personalized advertising.

These rights may be exercised through in-app consent dialogs or by contacting us directly.

Children’s Privacy

Our applications are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
